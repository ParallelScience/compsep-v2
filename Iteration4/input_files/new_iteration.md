- Decision: ITERATE
- Justification: The current results demonstrate that the SR-DAE is a highly effective tool for signal recovery, but the project is currently at a "Borderline" stage for a top-tier publication because it lacks a rigorous baseline comparison and a demonstration of physical robustness. The "Simulation-Specific" trap and the lack of a quantitative comparison against linear methods (ILC/Wiener filter) are the primary barriers to "Strong Accept" status. Implementing the suggested cross-simulation stress test and feature attribution (Saliency Maps) will transform the paper from a "demonstration of a model" to a "validated scientific instrument." The ROI of these improvements is high: they directly address the "So What?" factor by proving the model is learning physical gas dynamics rather than just performing pattern matching. Given the existing infrastructure and the high performance already achieved, these changes are technically feasible and will significantly strengthen the manuscript's impact on the cosmology community.
